Patronato Description

Patronato is a lively food hub that focuses on Arabic and Far Eastern cuisines, ranging from Korean barbecue and sushi to falafel.

The upper level of the Mercado de Abastos Tirso de Molina farmers' market is packed with down-to-earth Latin American restaurants.

At the central Vega market, stalls filled with produce and seafood can be found, while Asian supermarket stores and shops selling clothing and accessories are located in the mural-filled area.
